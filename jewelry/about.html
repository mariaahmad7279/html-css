About Us

Our Story

It all started with a passion

Founded in 1998, Ultra Jewelry is the culmination of a lifelong love affair with jewelry. What began as a small family endeavor has blossomed into a thriving online destination for those who appreciate the finer things in life.

Our journey started with a single mission: to provide our customers with exquisite, high-quality jewelry that captures the essence of special moments. Over the years, we’ve expanded our offerings to include a wide range of styles, from classic and timeless pieces to contemporary and trendy designs. Each piece in our collection is handpicked for its exceptional craftsmanship and beauty.
